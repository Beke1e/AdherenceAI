About the App

📱 Medication Adherence Prediction is essential for managing chronic conditions like Type 2 Diabetes. This AI-powered app predicts and monitors medication adherence levels in 🇪🇹 Ethiopian patients for better management.

🧠 Model Training

📊 Data was collected from 📍 University of Gondar Specialized Hospital. The General Medication Adherence Scale (GMAS) was used for assessment. To balance data, SMOTE was applied, and after evaluating models, the SVM model was deployed.

✨ Key Features

  • 🔐 Access: Sign up | Log in
  • 📝 Input: Enter demographics & GMAS scores
  • 📊 Tracking: Real-time adherence status (🔴 Low | 🟢 High)
